About Derm Assist
Derm Assist is an AI-powered skincare application designed to help users analyze their skin conditions, track skin health progress, book dermatologist appointments, and receive personalized skincare recommendations. The application uses advanced image analysis technology to detect acne, dryness, oil levels, glow score, and hydration levels.
